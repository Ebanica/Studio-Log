    Painting #3: Work in Progress
  • Inspiration for My Painting

    • Image 3.1: 8:00 a.m. - 9:p.m., September 22, 2023: This was the start of my painting when I decided to emphasize the dissapearance of the Arctic ice cap.

    Image 3.2: 8:00 p.m. to 11:p.m., September 25, 2023: In this stage, I applied the first layer by using the blended technique.

    Image 3.3: 7:00 a.m. to 10:a.m., September 26, 2023: I applied the second layer of my painting by using different techniques, such as

    impasto, blended, washing, and dry brush to create different texture and tones.

    Image 3.4: 9:00 p.m. to 11:p.m. September 27, 2023: In this stage, I applied some black ink in the upper left corner of the painting.

    This painting is unfinished, and I should work more on details next week. My current strength is the use of washing technique.
